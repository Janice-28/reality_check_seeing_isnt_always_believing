Deepfake Detection Technology

Multi-Modal Analysis

Our system analyzes media across multiple dimensions using specialized techniques for each format. For images, we employ Error Level Analysis and noise pattern detection. For videos, we perform frame-by-frame temporal consistency analysis. For audio, we evaluate voice naturalness and pitch stability patterns.

AI-Powered Detection

Our platform leverages state-of-the-art deep learning models: Vision Transformer (ViT) for image analysis, InceptionV3 with bidirectional GRUs and attention mechanisms for video processing, and advanced audio waveform analysis. These models are trained on the DeepFake Detection Challenge dataset to identify synthetic media with high accuracy.

Forensic Technology

We combine AI predictions with digital forensic techniques to provide comprehensive analysis. Our system examines multiple indicators of manipulation, including face detection consistency, compression artifacts, and temporal anomalies. This multi-layered approach ensures robust detection across various deepfake generation methods.
